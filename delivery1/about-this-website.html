About This Website

Our mission

This website is created to help bridge the communication between diabetes patient and their clinicians. The patients can use this website to easily record their own health data. The clinicians can manage multiple patients and support them through positive messages.

How To Use

Most of this site functions required you to log in

Patients

Please contact your clinician for registration. The clinician will ask you for more details and fill the registration form. They then should give you your account information comprised of email and password. The email will be what you give them, but the password will be something basic like '123456'. Please login and change your password as soon as possible.

Clinicians

You can register directly on this website. You are also responsible for adding patients to your account to manage. After added the patient, you can specify which health data you want the patient to record. The website has features that allow you to send supportive messages and to be notified when patients has dangerous measurements.
